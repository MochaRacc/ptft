Thornsbury's New Bus Zoning for Operatos & Routes Scheme 2025

We're delivering a new bus network by October 2025.

 

Overview

Here at Transnet, we're committed to improving public transpoert everyday.

And we're more committed to fixing the bus network by intorducing new buses, new routes and new operators to keep the network running smoothly.

 

Transnet's plan with routes

From Mid-July until Late September this year, operators can choose to continue their existing routes or scrap them, but must operate new routes before the September deadline.

All operators can also can choose what buses operate those routes (except 901, 902 & 903 - routes designated to Optimus buses only) and how many can run on those routes.

 

What operators will be apart of the new scheme?

Exisitng operators like McKian's, Connect Bus Lines & CDC Thornsbury are included in this scheme, with additions of O'Connors, CDC Thornsbury's sister branch CDC Aldershot, Tallentyne & Stockton Bus Service and Whitfords to the current operators list.

 

Zoning

Operators will only be allowed to run in certain areas listed out in the zoning map (see below), McKian's will own a majority of the bus network, with smaller and larger areas operated by other operators.
